OUR GAMES

Train Games

Join our protagonist, Simon - a passenger without a ticket, on an exciting train adventure to get to the capital city. You will have to be smart in order to get a place to sleep during this long trip.

  • Talk to passengers, but wisely! Choices made in the dialogue system influence the plot and the ending especially. Beware - some words thrown may throw you off the train.
  • Can't open a door? Look at your inventory, and try to drag and drop things from it on a scene.
  • Explore level - point over things in the car, maybe some of them can be picked up, and some other objects are of different use.
  • Have you already finished the game once? Try a different way - there are 3 endings and different ways to finish levels.
    • Would you get to the capital and how? The choice is yours.
